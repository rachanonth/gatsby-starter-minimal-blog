ขนาดการใช้ยา Fluconazole ใน Vulvovaginal Candidiasis

ไม่แนะนำให้ใช้ในกรณีติดเชื้อ *C. glabrata* หรือ C. krusei*

Mild or moderate infection in immunocompetent patient: 
**Oral:** 150 mg as a single dose 

Severe infection or infection in immunocompromised patient: 
**Oral:** 150 mg every 72 hours for 2 or 3 doses 

Recurrent infection: 
**Oral:** 150 mg every 72 hours for 10 to 14 days, followed by 150 mg once weekly for 6 months

- 100 mg, 150 mg, or 200 mg every 72 hours for 3 doses, then 100 mg, 150 mg, or 200 mg once weekly for 6 months
